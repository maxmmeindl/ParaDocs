Strategic Advantages in Federal Court

  • Automatic Liability: 1,340-day delay is indefensible - no reasonable jury could find for FEMA
  • Discovery Power: Compel production of all RA records, emails, and policies
  • Jury Sympathy: Disabled veteran ignored for 3.7 years plays extremely well to jury
  • Precedent Value: Case could set important precedent for RA processing requirements
  • Settlement Leverage: Class certification threat drives significant settlement value
  • Injunctive Relief: Court can order systemic reforms and monitoring
  • Fee Shifting: FEMA pays attorneys' fees when plaintiff prevails
