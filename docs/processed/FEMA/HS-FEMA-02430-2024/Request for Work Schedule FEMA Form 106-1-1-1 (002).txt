SECTION II: Schedule Selection (Select one of the following options)

Compressed Work Schedule (CWS)

CWS is an 80-hour biweekly basic work requirement scheduled for fewer than 10 workdays.

Flexible Work Schedule (FWS)

Flexitour - A schedule where the employee works the normal 8-hour workday, but may select the 
arrival times within the hours of 7:00 a.m. to 9:30 a.m. (arrival) and 3:30 p.m. to 6:00 p.m. 
(departure).

Maxiflex - A schedule that contains core hours on fewer than 10 workdays in the biweekly pay 
period. The employee may vary the number of hours worked on a given workday (between 6 a.m. 
and 6 p.m.) or the number of hours worked each week.
