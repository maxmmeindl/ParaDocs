<span style="font-size:10.0pt"><a href="https://link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTayvg2I9xK1D.LnUYKjxtvQPJx/ppRTdPER/3s/link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTQt3g2I9nK3k.LtMEKKTx76WCm/4hpzSkCQ/3s/www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/vaccines/fully-vaccinated_archived.html" target="_blank"><span style="font-size:12.0pt">fully vaccinated</span></a></span>,
 or do not wish to disclose vaccination status within the <span style="font-size:10.0pt">
<a href="https://link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTayvg2I9xK1D.LnUYKjxtvQPJx/CS9ujDNE/3s/link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTQt3g2I9nK3k.LtMEKKTx76WCm/biTIl1RV/3s/dhs.servicenowservices.com/vss" target="_blank"><span style="font-size:12.0pt">Vaccine Status System</span></a></span> (VSS), to provide a negative COVID-19 test result within
 24 hours prior to entering a FEMA facility. <p></p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto">Our records indicate that you either have not submitted proof of vaccination or have not provided an attestation of full vaccination in the
<span style="font-size:10.0pt"><a href="https://link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTayvg2I9xK1D.LnUYKjxtvQPJx/CS9ujDNE/3s/link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTQt3g2I9nK3k.LtMEKKTx76WCm/biTIl1RV/3s/dhs.servicenowservices.com/vss" target="_blank"><span style="font-size:12.0pt">Vaccine Status System</span></a></span> (VSS) as of February 24,
 2022. This message is notice of your automatic and mandatory enrollment in FEMA’s COVID-19 Testing Program. If you are vaccinated, you are encouraged to update your status in VSS.<p></p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto">Employees are required to comply with established health and safety protocols, including mandatory
<span style="font-size:10.0pt"><a href="https://link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTayvg2I9xK1D.LnUYKjxtvQPJx/0po_YoXL/3s/link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTQt3g2I9nK3k.LtMEKKTx76WCm/ggWRrTb-/3s/usfema.sharepoint.com/sites/MS/ocao/oshe/ESHtlKit/Shared%20Documents/Forms/AllItems.aspx?id=%2Fsites%2FMS%2Focao%2Foshe%2FESHtlKit%2FShared%20Documents%2FCOVID%2D19%20Testing%20References%2FCOVID%2D19%20RTW%20Entry%20and%20Testing%20Procedures%20for%20All%20FEMA%20Controlled%20Sites%20FINAL%2002232022%2Epdf&amp;parent=%2Fsites%2FMS%2Focao%2Foshe%2FESHtlKit%2FShared%20Documents%2FCOVID%2D19%20Testing%20References" target="_blank"><span class="gmail-m-8292784115059787744m7004532798150327347msosmartlink"><span style="font-size:12.0pt">COVID-19

<p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto">To comply with FEMA COVID-19 testing protocol, employees that are not fully vaccinated must:<p></p></p>
<ul type="disc">
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo3">
Obtain a negative COVID-19 test result within 24 hours prior to entering a FEMA facility, or have
<span style="font-size:10.0pt"><a href="https://link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTayvg2I9xK1D.LnUYKjxtvQPJx/530vYchB/3s/link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTQt3g2I9nK3k.LtMEKKTx76WCm/aroqfIzg/3s/usfema.sharepoint.com/sites/MS/FF/Communications%20Library/03.01.21%20Updated%20CDC%20Guidance%20on%20Vaccinated%20Persons,%20Quarantine,%20and%20COVID-19%20Testing%20.pdf" target="_blank"><span class="gmail-m-8292784115059787744m7004532798150327347msosmartlink"><span style="font-size:12.0pt">proof
 of 90-day testing exemption</span></span></a></span> following COVID-19 diagnosis and recovery.<p></p></li><li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo3">

<p></p></li></ul>
<ul type="disc">

<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto;mso-list:l2 level2 lfo4">

</ul>
<ul type="disc">
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto;mso-list:l3 level1 lfo5">
Notify your supervisor and the FEMA Contact Tracing Team if a positive COVID-19 result is obtained. Employees who test positive will not be permitted access to a FEMA facility.
<p></p></li></ul>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto"><span style="font-size:2.0pt">&nbsp;</span><p></p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto">Test kits are available at no cost to individuals who have not provided proof of vaccination for the purposes of entry to FEMA facilities and those who require tests for official

<span style="font-size:10.0pt"><a href="https://link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTayvg2I9xK1D.LnUYKjxtvQPJx/nhr6aY_l/3s/link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTQt3g2I9nK3k.LtMEKKTx76WCm/xntXJ9Je/3s/usfema.sharepoint.com/sites/MS/ocao/oshe/ESHtlKit/Shared%20Documents/Forms/AllItems.aspx?id=%2Fsites%2FMS%2Focao%2Foshe%2FESHtlKit%2FShared%20Documents%2FEmployee%20Resources%2FFEMA%20Safety%20Officials%20Contact%20List%2Epdf&amp;parent=%2Fsites%2FMS%2Focao%2Foshe%2FESHtlKit%2FShared%20Documents%2FEmployee%20Resources" target="_blank"><span class="gmail-m-8292784115059787744m7004532798150327347msosmartlink"><span style="font-size:12.0pt">Safety
 Officer</span></span></a></span>.&nbsp; <p></p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto">Alternatively, employees may also obtain an approved third-party COVID-19 test from a physician, medical clinic, or hospital, if the results are on official letterhead with the
 name of the facility, the name of the physician or other licensed healthcare professional administering the test, results of the test, and date.&nbsp; Reimbursement for use of personal funds for third-party tests are not authorized, except for the limited circumstances
 as outlined by the <span style="font-size:10.0pt"><a href="https://link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTayvg2I9xK1D.LnUYKjxtvQPJx/2M1eUxkM/3s/link.dradvocates.com/click/1nTQt3g2I9nK3k.LtMEKKTx76WCm/J6_8D-3f/3s/usfema.sharepoint.com/teams/OPPAPolicy/Lists/Policy%20Master%20List/Attachments/918/Instruction%20-%20Medical%20Evaluation%20Program%20COVID-19%20Testing.pdf" target="_blank"><span class="gmail-m-8292784115059787744m7004532798150327347msosmartlink"><span style="font-size:12.0pt">Medical

 text your&nbsp;zip code&nbsp;to&nbsp;438829, or call&nbsp;1-800-232-0233&nbsp;to find COVID-19 vaccine locations near you.<p></p></p>
