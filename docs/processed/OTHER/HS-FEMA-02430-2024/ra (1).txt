According to policy:
FEMA Manual 1430.1 Chg.1 December 3, 2002
4-4
Chapter 5
Time Frames
5-1. Processing Requests. FEMA will process requests for reasonable accommodation and provide accommodations, where they are appropriate, in as short a time frame as reasonably possible. FEMA recognizes, however, that the time necessary to process a request will depend on the nature of the accommodation requested and whether it is necessary to obtain supporting information. Time frames for processing requests and providing reasonable accommodation where no supporting information is required are as follows:
a. Requests from applicants for employment should be expedited when necessary to ensure the applicant's ability to compete for the position. However, these requests should not exceed ten (10) business days.
b. If a request from an employee is processed by the supervisor, the request should be processed, and the accommodation, if granted, provided within 15 business days from the date of receipt of the request. Requests for accommodation should be expedited when the accommodation is needed to enable the employee to participate in an Agency activity scheduled to occur in the near future.
(1) If the decision maker believes that it is necessary to obtain medical information to determine whether the requesting individual has a disability and/or to identify the functional limitations, the decision maker will request the information as soon as possible after his or her receipt of the request for accommodation. FEMA recognizes that the need for documentation may not become apparent until after the interactive process has begun.
(2) In cases where medical documentation is needed, the accommodation, if granted, will be provided within ten (10) business days for an applicant, and within 15 business days for an employee, from the date the decision maker receives the relevant information, absent any extenuating circumstances.
c. Where the Disability Program Manager is the decision maker on a request for reasonable accommodation, he/she will make a decision on the request and the  accommodation, if granted, will be provided within 15 business days from the date of the request. If medical documentation is necessary, the decision will be made within 15 business days of receipt of the medical information, absent any extenuating circumstances.

We are going on 132 business days since AUGUST 23rd 2018 and 196 calendar days.
Unreasonable and unnecessary.

<p class="MsoNormal"><b><i><u><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Calibri Light&quot;,sans-serif;color:#1F4E79">According to policy:<o:p></o:p></span></u></i></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Calibri Light&quot;,sans-serif;color:#1F4E79">FEMA Manual 1430.1 Chg.1 December 3, 2002<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Calibri Light&quot;,sans-serif;color:#1F4E79">4-4<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Calibri Light&quot;,sans-serif;color:#1F4E79">Chapter 5<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Calibri Light&quot;,sans-serif;color:#1F4E79">Time Frames<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Calibri Light&quot;,sans-serif;color:#1F4E79">5-1. Processing Requests. FEMA will process requests for reasonable accommodation and provide accommodations, where they are appropriate, in as short
 a time frame as reasonably possible. FEMA recognizes, however, that the time necessary to process a request will depend on the nature of the accommodation requested and whether it is necessary to obtain supporting information. Time frames for processing requests
 and providing reasonable accommodation where no supporting information is required are as follows:<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Calibri Light&quot;,sans-serif;color:#1F4E79">a. Requests from applicants for employment should be expedited when necessary to ensure the applicant&#8217;s ability to compete for the position. However,
 these requests should not exceed ten (10) business days.<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Calibri Light&quot;,sans-serif;color:#1F4E79">b. If a request from an employee is processed by the supervisor, the request should be processed, and the accommodation, if granted,
<b><u>provided within 15 business days</u></b> from the date of receipt of the request. Requests for accommodation should be expedited when the accommodation is needed to enable the employee to participate in an Agency activity scheduled to occur in the near
 future.<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Calibri Light&quot;,sans-serif;color:#1F4E79">(1) If the decision maker believes that it is necessary to obtain medical information to determine whether the requesting individual has a disability
 and/or to identify the functional limitations, the decision maker will request the information as soon as possible after his or her receipt of the request for accommodation. FEMA recognizes that the need for documentation may not become apparent until after
 the interactive process has begun.<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Calibri Light&quot;,sans-serif;color:#1F4E79">(2) In cases where medical documentation is needed, the accommodation, if granted, will be
<b><u>provided within ten (10) business days</u></b> for an applicant, and within
<b><u>15 business days for an employee</u></b>, from the date the decision maker receives the relevant information, absent any extenuating circumstances.<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><i><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:&quot;Calibri Light&quot;,sans-serif;color:#1F4E79">c. Where the Disability Program Manager is the decision maker on a request for reasonable accommodation, he/she will make a decision on the request and
 the &nbsp;accommodation, if granted, will be provided <b><u>within 15 business days from the date of the request</u></b>. If medical documentation is necessary, the decision will be made within
<b><u>15 business days of receipt of the medical information</u></b>, absent any extenuating circumstances.<o:p></o:p></span></i></p>
